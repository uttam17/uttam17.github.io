Good Design Bad Design

In this project, the goal was to find one example of a good design and one of a bad design on Bucknell University's website. I was tasked with explaining through text and images why I preferred one design and why I disliked the other. Click the title to learn more.

Design for Fun

We took advantage of Leap Motion technology in this design sprint to create a new interface for one of our favorite online games. We converted Ice Breaker so the user can use Leap controllers to interact with the game and save the day. Click the title to learn more.

Design for Wellbeing

Design for Wellbeing

This was by far my favorite design sprint and final prototype of all seven. We worked to create a website that promoted wellbeing using Affectiva's video processing libraries. We tracked users emotion and presented them with memes, gifs, and quotes based on their facial emotion. Click the title and find out more!
